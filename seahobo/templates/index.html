Accommodation description of boat layout

The layout features over & under berths forward including a hanging locker.

Next aft to port is the head compartment with a separate shower area, and opposite to starboard is a generous hanging locker with additional drawer storage.

The main salon features a L-shaped dinette to port including a fixed table, and across to starboard is a settee with storage outboard.

Aft to port is the U-shaped galley, and across to starboard is the navigation station followed by the quarter berth.

There is excellent storage throughout the interior, and five opening port lights and two opening deck hatches provide plenty of ventilation and natural light.
